7. Your Rights Under KVKK

Under Article 11 of KVKK, you have the following rights:

  • Learning whether your personal data is being processed
  • Requesting information about processed data
  • Learning the purpose of processing and whether it is used accordingly
  • Knowing the third parties to whom data is transferred domestically or abroad
  • Requesting correction of incomplete or incorrectly processed data
  • Requesting deletion or destruction of data under the conditions of Article 7 of KVKK
  • Requesting notification of correction and deletion operations to third parties
  • Objecting to decisions made against you through automated systems
  • Requesting compensation for damages

You can submit your requests via email, postal mail, or in person. Your request will be concluded within 30 days. If you are not satisfied with the response, you can file a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Authority.
